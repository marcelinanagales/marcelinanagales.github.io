Professionally, I am the Software Team Manager for the Research Computing Center at Florida State University. I am  also a specialist in Digital Humanities methods and methodologies. My research interests include digital reconstruction, digital preservation through 3D models of cultural heritage objects, and teaching programming in the context of Digital Humanities. Since 2018, I has been a Lab Manager for the Morphometrics Lab at FSU and provided technical support for The Demos Center.
